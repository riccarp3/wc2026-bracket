⚙️ Rules & Creative Wrinkles
Phase 1 Deadline: Group stage picks must be submitted before June 11, 2026 at 3:00 PM ET (tournament kickoff). Group picks lock automatically.
Phase 2 Opens: The knockout bracket opens June 28, 2026 once the Round of 32 matchups are confirmed. All 32 qualified teams appear in every dropdown — pick your way from R32 to the Final.
3rd-place wildcards: The 8 best third-place teams advance to the R32. Those slots are TBD until group results are known — you'll still pick the R32 winner either way.
The Penalty Shootout Wildcard 🎲: Once per tournament, each participant may "call a shootout" on any knockout match they got wrong. If that match actually went to penalties, you recover half the points (rounded down). One per person — use it wisely.
Team-building twist: Consider randomly assigning each participant a national team and awarding a separate prize to whoever's assigned team goes furthest — totally independent of the main bracket competition.
